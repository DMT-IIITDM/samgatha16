STARTUPEDIA
Startupedia
STARTUPEDIA presented by IIITD&M in association with Srushty Global Solutions is a gathering of students and professionals from the start up fraternity. It is an interactive session wherein eminent panelists will take up questions from students and express their opinion. Discussion will be held on the following topics:
  • Startup India
  • Mentorship and investment for Startups
  • Working for Startups
  • Cooperative Development of Startups
  • Incubation of Ideas
PANELIST
Maruthachala Ponnambalam
Director at Srushty Global Solutions Pvt Ltd

Mauthachala Ponnambalam Graduated with B.Tech Mechanical from IIT Kanpur in 1994. He then joined Tata Motors as a graduate trainee engineer and later worked for HCL Technologies. He is currently owns and runs Srushty Global Solutions, which is a company focussing on product development and engineering services.

PANELIST
Prasanna Jagannathan
CEO of Brigge

Prasanna Jagannathan (PJ) is presently the CEO of Brigge, the first-of-its-kind social network for real world activities. He cofounded Brigge along with Sam and Murali with the aim of using technology to bring back real world socialising. Prior to starting Brigge, PJ was leading operational excellence and mergers & integrations programs at Silicon Valley Corporations. After 10+ years in the US, PJ, who is a first-generation entrepreneur, returned to India to get back to his roots and make a global impact with a world-class Indian product.
